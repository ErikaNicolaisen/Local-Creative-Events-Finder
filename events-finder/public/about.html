About StageUp CPH

StageUp CPH is a platform for finding volunteer opportunities at Copenhagen's best festivals and music venues. Whether you want to work with sound, lighting, photography or stage management — StageUp helps you find the right shift and build real experience in the live music industry.

Volunteering is one of the best ways to break into the music and events industry. You get hands-on experience, meet professionals, and become part of Copenhagen's vibrant cultural scene — all while giving back to the community.

Opportunities are scraped directly from festival and venue websites, so you always get the latest shifts and roles available.

What you can do

  • Browse volunteer opportunities on the map
  • Like and save shifts you're interested in
  • Filter by category — stage, sound, lighting or photography
  • Post your own volunteer opportunity
